Returns & Exchanges Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, here's our policy for returns and exchanges:

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Products must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging
  • Customized kits (with player names/numbers) cannot be returned unless defective
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@soccerxclusive.com with your order number
  2. We'll send you a return authorization and instructions
  3. Package your items securely with the original packing slip
  4. Ship your return using a tracked shipping service
  5. Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return

Exchanges

For exchanges, simply follow the return process and place a new order for the item you want. This ensures you get your new item as quickly as possible.

Defective Items

If you receive a defective item, please contact us immediately with photos of the defect. We'll arrange a replacement or refund and cover return shipping costs.

Measuring Tips

  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure horizontal
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline
  • For jerseys, we recommend sizing up if you're between sizes for a more comfortable fit
  • Most football jerseys are designed with an athletic fit

Still unsure? Contact our customer service team for personalized sizing advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also log into your account on our website and view your order history to track your package.

What if my kit doesn't fit?

If your kit doesn't fit, you can return it within 30 days of purchase for an exchange or refund, as long as it's unworn and in original packaging. Please refer to our Returns Policy for detailed instructions on how to process your return.

How do I care for my football kit?

To maintain the quality of your kit, we recommend washing in cold water on a gentle cycle, turning the kit inside out before washing, avoiding bleach and fabric softeners, and air drying instead of using a dryer. Do not iron directly on prints or badges.

Can I modify my order after it's placed?

We process orders quickly to ensure fast shipping. If you need to modify your order, please contact our customer service team immediately. We'll do our best to accommodate changes, but we cannot guarantee modifications once the order processing has begun.

Do you offer price matching?

Yes, we offer price matching for identical products found at lower prices from authorized retailers. To request a price match, please contact our customer service team with proof of the lower price before placing your order or within 7 days of purchase.

What if my package is lost or damaged?

If your package appears to be lost or arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team immediately. For lost packages, we'll work with the shipping carrier to locate it or process a replacement. For damaged items, please send photos of the damage, and we'll arrange a replacement or refund.
