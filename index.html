Frequently Asked Questions

Are the kits authentic?

Yes, all our kits are authentic reproductions that match the quality and design of official team merchandise. While not manufactured by the official team suppliers, our kits are made with premium materials and attention to detail.

What sizes are available?

We offer sizes ranging from Small to XXL for adult kits, and ages 4-14 for youth kits. Detailed size charts are available on each product page to help you find the perfect fit.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by location. Standard shipping within the US typically takes 5-7 business days, while international shipping can take 10-15 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout.

Can I return or exchange my kit?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in original packaging. Exchanges are also available if you need a different size. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return or exchange.

Can I add custom names and numbers?

Absolutely! We offer customization services for most kits. You can add your favorite player's name and number, or your own personalized text. Simply select the customization option during checkout and provide the details.
